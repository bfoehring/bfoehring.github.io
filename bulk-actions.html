Bulk actions give users the ability to work through their inbox and complete repetitive tasks in bulk. This saves users time and ultimately makes their job easier.

Bulk Actions
The Problem

Users are combing through potentially thousands of messages related to their brand daily. Each tiny interaction while engaging with these messages, even if only seconds long, can add up over the course of a work day. I was tasked with creating an experience that would simplify users' workflows by streamlining tedious tasks and refund them precious time.

The Work

The notion that Sprout was costing our users valuable time was a bit alarming. With that in mind, I immediately set out to create some flows and rough wireframes for an MVP that could be released as soon as possible. With a plan in place and work on an MVP in progress, I immediately set out to document a future version of the project. This would allow bulk actions to evolve into a more mature and valuable feature.

Finally, I was able to prototype the vision for bulk complete to conduct user tests to gain valuable insights that would ultimately lead to a better final product in later stages of the feature. The tests were successful and the vision now stands as the accepted future of bulk actions for Sprout Social.

Compose is the tool with which Sprout user's compose messages for posting to the social networks.

Compose Prototype
The Problem

The goal of this project was to create a strategy which would allow us to move away from our old compose which was consumed in tech debt and into a new scalable compose. All the while continuing to release features and value to customers.

The Work

Compose at Sprout had almost become a right of passage. It was riddled with design and tech debt and prevented our product team from being able to truly push the experience without a full rebuild. The themes I used to drive my design were, familiarity, cleanliness, modularity, scalability, and productivity. I wanted to ensure that users received the best possible experience to compliment their workflows while ensuring that the technical situation we had ourselves in never happened again.

Some key features include:

  • A button group that allowed users to toggle tools and views to preserve vertical screen real estate.
  • A modular, scalable, fluid experience that catered to a number of different personas by allowing them to choose the way they interacted with compose.
  • Enhancements to all exisiting features such as scheduling and queueing to streamline workflows.
  • A notification center to alert users of cross network conflicts and other background processes.
  • A refreshed UI to improve legibilty and make writing a pleasure.
  • A comprehensive launch strategy that allowed us to incrementally improve compose while releasing features in parallel.

Landscape

Landscape is an image resizing tool for social media marketers that streamlines the arduous task of resizing an images or images for use across the many social networks. Landscape was born out of Sprout's hackweek and evolved into a free and popular tool that aids in not only solving a customer problem, but driving new users to Sprout.

The Problem

Our team was faced with the challenge of researching the sizes available from the networks as well as how to build a configurator that complimented the typical manager's workflow. Our process began with researching sizes and workflows and transitioned into whiteboarding the user's journey.

The Work

The Landscape brand was inspired by the organic nature of "Sprout" and the art of cropping images. The logo, lock-up, and logotype all include the trademark cropping tool containing line as well as the Landscape "L" set in Gotham Rounded. The colors and imagery, drawn from nature, provide beautiful visuals and pay homage to Sprout.

The goal of Landscape's UI/UX was to provide a seemingly simple and friendly approach to an otherwise daunting task. A soft aesthetic, visual aids, and large click targets, set in a transparent step by step process, helped us achieve just that.
